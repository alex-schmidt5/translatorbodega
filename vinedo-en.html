Viñedo

Our vineyard, spanning 6 hectares, was planted in October 2012 and gave us its first harvest in 2015. Its average annual production varies between 18,000 and 26,000 bottles.

On the left side of the vineyard, 3 hectares of Malbec stretch out, and on the right, 20 more rows of the same variety. Then we continue with rows of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Petit Verdot, and Cabernet Franc.

We use drip irrigation and the 2x1 espalier training system. The harvest is done manually: for Chardonnay and Pinot Noir, it takes place at the end of February, while the other red varieties are harvested between March and April.
